The Vampire Diaries and The Originals

Official Reunion Convention

As we have had to postpone or cancel many of our shows in 2020, the task of finding available venues in 2021 has been very challenging. But we’re very lucky to have found an amazing venue at the Renaissance Schaumburg Hotel so we can now present The Vampire Diaries/Originals Reunion Convention in the Chicago area in 2021!

The love and support that we’ve received over the years from our loyal Vampire Diaries fandom has warmed even the coldest of vampire hearts! Giving attendees the chance to interact with all their favorite TVD stars from the franchise, including The Originals and Legacies, has been a great pleasure.

The best part is we are returning to one of our favorite destinations! Being near the great city of Chicago always provides some fun-filled vacation opportunities. Make sure to check out some of the sites and attractions that Chicago has to offer to add to your fab weekend with our fellow TVD family!
